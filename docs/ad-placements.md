# Ad Placement Recommendations

Recommendations for non-intrusive ad placements across IndiaMovieGuide pages.

---

## Home Page

**1. Between Hero and "New Releases" — Leaderboard**
Highest-traffic slot on the site. The hero ends and there is a natural content break before the first movie grid. A `728×90` leaderboard (or `970×90` wide leaderboard on desktop) sits here cleanly without interrupting any browsing flow.

**2. Between "Upcoming Releases" and "Genres" — Medium Rectangle**
Natural break between two major sections. A `300×250` centered rectangle works well here, or a `728×90` if a thinner horizontal break is preferred.

---

## Movie Detail Page

**3. Right Sidebar — below "Ratings & Reviews" — Half Page or Medium Rectangle**
The sidebar (`.movie-side`, 360px wide) has a natural end after ratings. A `300×600` half-page or `300×250` rectangle here is the most premium slot on the site — high-intent users who have clicked into a specific movie. The sidebar width comfortably fits both sizes.

**4. Between "Songs" and "Cast" — Leaderboard**
Full-width section break. A `728×90` leaderboard between the songs list and the cast grid is unobtrusive and catches users scrolling down for more info.

---

## Browse / Language / Genre Pages

**5. After the page header, before the movie grid — Leaderboard**
There is already a header section with a heading, tagline, and nav chips. A `728×90` below those chips and above the grid feels natural — similar to how Google Images and similar sites place ads before result grids.

---

## Streaming Page

**6. Below provider filter bar — Leaderboard**
After the provider pills row (which users interact with to filter), a `728×90` before the movie grid is a clean, expected placement.

---

## Person / Actor Page

**7. Between Bio and Filmography grid — Medium Rectangle**
Natural break between the hero (photo + bio) and the full filmography grid below. A `300×250` centered here works well on a page that otherwise has no obvious ad slots.

---

## Recommended Sizes Summary

| Slot | Size | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Home → between hero and New Releases | `728×90` leaderboard | Widest natural break, high visibility |
| Home → between Upcoming and Genres | `300×250` medium rectangle | Centered, non-blocking |
| Movie detail → sidebar | `300×600` half page | Premium slot, sidebar is exactly 360px wide |
| Movie detail → between songs and cast | `728×90` leaderboard | Full-width break, low friction |
| Browse / Language pages → above grid | `728×90` leaderboard | Standard search-results pattern |
| Streaming page → below filter | `728×90` leaderboard | Filter interaction already breaks flow |
| Person page → between bio and filmography | `300×250` medium rectangle | Only natural break on the page |

**For mobile**, swap any `728×90` with `320×50` (mobile banner) or `320×100` (large mobile banner), and replace `300×600` with `300×250`.

---

## What to Avoid

- Inside the movie hero / poster area — breaks visual design
- Between song rows or cast cards — interrupts browsing
- Inside the nav bar or footer
- Interstitials / overlays — the site's value is fast info access, popups will hurt retention
